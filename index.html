What day was it?

Name the day of any birthday — in your head!

🍪 The big idea

Do two easy mini-sums and remember the leftover from each. Then add those two leftovers — and the answer is the day!

⬇ count out the 7s one last time
leftover = the day ⭐
🚀 Years 2000+: take away 1 in Part 2 before counting 7s.   🐸 Leap years: use 0 for January and 3 for February (other months stay the same).
